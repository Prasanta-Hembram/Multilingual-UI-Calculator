Hi, This is a MultiScript Simple Calculator, where even the numbers and output also get translated into their respective language (If that language has its own writing system otherwise the numbers will always be in English numerals by default). These are some lists of all the languages which are currently supported, please feel free to add your language too if it is missing here.

Click on any one of the locales below or choose L10N from the top left button to select your localized calculator.

  • Bengali
  • English
  • Hindi(Devanagari)
  • Odiya
  • Santali(Ol Chiki)
